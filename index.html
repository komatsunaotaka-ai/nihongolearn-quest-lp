Questions parents ask

You've got questions. We've got answers.

When will NihongoQuest launch?

We're targeting Q3 2026 for the official launch on the App Store and Google Play. Waitlist members get early access 2 weeks before public launch.

Does my child need to know any Japanese to start?

No prior knowledge needed. We start from the very first hiragana character and build up step by step. The app also supports complete beginners all the way up to JLPT N3 level.

Is it safe for children? Are there ads?

Yes, it's designed for kids. No ads, no in-app purchases inside lessons, no chat with strangers. You can set screen-time limits and get weekly progress reports. We comply with Malaysia's PDPA for child data.

What languages is the app interface in?

English, Bahasa Malaysia, and Simplified Chinese. Grammar explanations are available in all three languages.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions can be cancelled anytime through the App Store or Google Play. We also offer a 7-day free trial — no commitment.

How is this different from Duolingo?

We're built specifically for Malaysian K-12 learners. That means local-language support (BM, Chinese, English), cultural sensitivity (halal-friendly content), Malaysian pricing, and a focus on speaking — with AI conversation partners that give real pronunciation feedback, something Duolingo doesn't offer for Japanese.
